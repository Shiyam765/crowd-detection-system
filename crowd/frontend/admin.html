Density Classification Guide

Based on your active Current limits, crowds are classified as follows:

What are these thresholds?

⚠️ Warning Threshold: Represents the starting boundary for crowd build-ups. When counts cross this level, status turns yellow (Moderate density), indicating crowds are forming and should be observed closely.

🚨 Critical Threshold: The danger limit. Once crossed, the status turns red (High density) and immediate alarms or emails are fired. Safety interventions are recommended at this point.

🔮 Predicted Thresholds (10-Min): Used to trigger early-warning alerts *proactively*. If the 10-minute trend prediction indicates the crowd is accelerating fast enough to exceed the predicted critical threshold within 10 minutes, the system flags it early so you can react before it's too late.
